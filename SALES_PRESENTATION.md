- Clean, modern, professional
- High-quality coffee shop photography
- Subtle gradients and shadows
- Consistent icon style (line icons or filled)
- Plenty of white space
- Avoid clutter

### Image Recommendations:
- Latte art close-ups
- Happy customers with coffee
- Modern coffee shop interiors
- Baristas at work
- Mobile devices showing the app
- Dashboard screenshots (actual)

### Transitions:
- Keep them subtle (fade, push)
- Don't overuse animations
- Professional, not flashy
- 0.5-0.7 second duration
