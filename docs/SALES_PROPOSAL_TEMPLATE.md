## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if my customers don't use online ordering?
**A:** We've seen 20-40% adoption within the first 3 months across similar coffee shops. Even at the low end, that's significant additional revenue. Plus, the system pays for itself with just 10-15 orders per day.

### Q: What about delivery?
**A:** The current system is pickup-focused, which keeps costs low. Delivery integration is available as an add-on if needed in the future.

### Q: Can I update the menu myself?
**A:** Yes! Your employee dashboard includes a menu management interface where you can add items, change prices, and mark things as unavailable.

### Q: What if something breaks?
**A:** We provide ongoing support and monitor the system 24/7. In the rare event of an issue, we'll fix it quickly and keep you informed.

### Q: Do my employees need special training?
**A:** The system is very intuitive. We provide a 2-hour training session, and most staff are comfortable using it within 30 minutes.

### Q: What about customers without smartphones?
**A:** The website works on any device - smartphone, tablet, or computer. Customers without internet access can still order in-person or by phone as they do now.

### Q: Can customers save their payment information?
**A:** Yes, registered customers can securely save payment methods through Stripe for faster checkout in the future.

### Q: What happens if we want to cancel?
**A:** We offer month-to-month subscriptions with no long-term contract. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
